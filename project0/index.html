What is Youtube?

Youtube is a video sharing service where you can watch, like, share, comment and upload your own videos.

Why I like Youtube?

I like Youtube because you can find almost anything in it. From tutorials about how to do basic chores, for someone that lives alone for the first time, to lectures about scientific topics that can help you in your university milestone project. With Youtube you can find a more interactive way of learning, in case it is easiest for you to learn with visual support. There are tons of movies and books reviews and people making funny videos. In general, I like Youtube cause you can find almost anything there.
